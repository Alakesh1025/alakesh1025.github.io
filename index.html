About Me

Hi, I'm Alakesh Kalita, currently working with Dr. Mohan Gurusamy as a Reserach Fellow in Electrical and Computer Engineering department of National University of Singapore, Singapore. Prior to this, I received my Ph.D. with the department of Computer Science and Engineering in Indian Institute of Technology Guwahati, India, under the supervision of Dr. Manas Khatua. During my Ph.D., I worked on Adaptive Resource Allocation for Faster Formation of 6TiSCH IoT Network . My Ph.D. thesis is available here.

I received the Innovative Student Projects Award 2022 (equivalent to best thesis) from Indian National Academy of Engineering (INAE) for my Ph.D. Thesis .

It's been more than 7 years, I'm working in the field of Internet of Things. Back in 2016, I worked in an Indian government-sponsored project titled "Quality of Service Provisioning in Internet of Things" as a research scientist. Later, in 2017, I worked in Internet of Vehicle in IIIT Guwahati.

My current research interests include Internet of Things, Game Theory in Computer Networks
